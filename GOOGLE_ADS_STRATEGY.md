**Keywords:**

## Keyword Research

Use Google Keyword Planner:
1. Low competition, high volume keywords
2. Location modifiers (Toronto, Mississauga, etc.)
3. Long-tail keywords
4. Seasonal variations

Popular keywords (HIGH-QUALITY ONLY):
- sell my car toronto (high volume, high intent)
- cash for cars (high intent)
- car valuation toronto (mid funnel)
- sell car fast (high intent)
- quick car sale (high intent)
- instant car offer (mid funnel)
- sell my honda (model-specific, high intent)
- car buyer toronto (high intent)

⚠️ **NEVER use these:**
- sell junk car (attracts non-running vehicles)
- damaged car valuation (attracts totaled vehicles)
- scrap car removal (attracts metal/parts buyers)
- car for parts (not our target)
